Studio

DesignMist is a full-service architecture and interior design studio based in Amravati, Maharashtra. We work at the intersection of functionality and beauty — crafting spaces that feel as good as they look.

From initial concept to final finish, we engage deeply with every client to understand not just what they want, but how they want to live, work, and feel. That understanding is the foundation of every design decision.

Our portfolio spans residential homes, commercial complexes, hospitality interiors, and retail environments — each project bringing a fresh eye and a commitment to enduring quality.
